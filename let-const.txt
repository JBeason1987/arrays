Quiz
What is the difference between var and let? You can redeclare var but you can not with let.
What is the difference between var and const? You can reassign/redeclare var but not with const.
What is the difference between let and const? You can reassign with let, but not with const.
What is hoisting? The order in which code is structured in the codebase. Hoisted functions trigger before they are defined in the codebase.
